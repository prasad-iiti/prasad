## About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher at IIT Indore and an Assistant Professor at SGGSIE&T Nanded.
My research focuses on **deep learning for medical image analysis**, particularly **skin lesion classification** for early detection of dermatological diseases.

My work targets the design of:

* Computationally efficient AI models
* Real-time deployable healthcare systems
* Robust learning under class imbalance and data variability
* Trustworthy and interpretable AI for clinical adoption
